## Overview

This onboarding program ensures new employees become proficient with the Multi-Business Management System within 4 weeks.

**Program Structure:**
- **Duration:** 4 weeks (20 business days)
- **Time Commitment:** ~3 hours/day on training
- **Format:** Self-paced learning + hands-on exercises
- **Assessment:** Knowledge checks at each week
- **Certification:** Final practical exam

**Learning Objectives:**
- Navigate the system confidently
- Perform core job functions independently
- Understand business-specific features
- Handle common issues and troubleshooting
- Follow best practices and workflows
