# 🧸 Toddlers’ Clothing Categories

## 👕 Everyday Basics

### 🧾 Casual Clothing

- 👕 T-shirts (short- and long-sleeve)
- 👖 Soft pants (joggers, pull-on pants)
- 🩳 Shorts
- 👗 Simple dresses/rompers (for girls or gender-neutral)
- 🧶 Sweaters and pullovers
- 🧥 Lightweight zip-up hoodies or cardigans
- 🧦 Socks and booties
- 👒 Sun hats and beanies


## 🛌 Sleep \& Lounge

### 🛏️ Sleepwear

- 🌙 One-piece sleepers/footed pajamas
- 👕 Pajama sets (top + pants)
- 🩳 Sleep shorts (warmer climates)
- 🧣 Sleep sacks/wearable blankets (infants-to-toddlers transition)
- 🧦 Non-slip bedtime socks


## 🩲 Underwear \& Toileting

### 🚽 Potty / Training Items

- 🩲 Training underwear (pull-ups)
- 🩲 Underwear (beginning stages)
- 🩳 Swim diapers / reusable swim pants
- 🧴 Undershirts and camisoles
- 🧷 Simple undershirt layers


## 🧥 Outerwear \& Weather Gear

### 🌦️ Weather-specific Pieces

- 🧥 Light jackets and windbreakers
- 🧥 Insulated coats and puffer jackets
- 🌧️ Raincoats and ponchos
- 🧤 Mittens and gloves
- 🧣 Scarves and neck warmers
- 🥾 Rain boots and insulated boots


## 🎽 Active \& Playwear

### 🤸 Durable Play Clothing

- 👕 Play tees and tanks
- 👖 Reinforced-knee pants/overalls
- 🧥 Waterproof bibs and smocks for messy play
- 👟 Slip-on shoes and sneakers suitable for learning to walk
- 🧢 Sun-protective clothing (UPF hats, rash guards)


## 👔 Special \& Dressy

### 🎉 Occasions and Photos

- 👗 Party dresses and outfits
- 🧥 Mini blazers or dress shirts
- 👖 Dress pants and skirts
- 👞 Dress shoes and ballet flats
- 🎀 Bows, headbands, and small ties


## 👟 Footwear

### 👣 Shoes by stage

- 👶 Soft-soled crib shoes (early walkers)
- 👟 First-walker sneakers with flexible soles
- 🥾 Boots (sizes with easy closures)
- 🩴 Sandals with heel straps
- 🥿 Slip-on and Velcro shoes for independence


## 👜 Accessories \& Extras

### ✨ Useful Add-ons

- 🧢 Sun hats and caps
- 🧣 Bibs (feeding and drool bibs)
- 🧳 Small backpacks or daycare baggies
- 🧷 Socks, shoe liners, and replacement fasteners (Velcro)
- 🧼 Clothing repair kit: spare buttons, safety pins
